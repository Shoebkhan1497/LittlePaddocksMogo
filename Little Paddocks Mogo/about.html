About Us

This is not just a place you visit.

Little Paddocks is something you step into, a slow, handmade and welcoming world of tea, creativity and connection.

Our story

A creative wellness space in Mogo.

Little Paddocks brings people together through tea, seasonal making, small workshops, retreats and storytelling. The space is grounded in nature, handmade textures and meaningful connection.

Visitors may come for tea, Create ritual pottery painting, a quiet gathering, an Alchemy session or a Playback Theatre experience. Each offering is designed to feel personal, calm and authentic.

  • Slow and sensory experiences
  • Tea, flowers, clay and natural textures
  • Small-group workshops and community events
  • Creativity as a pathway to wellbeing

Our feeling

Warm. Grounded. Calm.

The website and brand should feel authentic rather than polished, simple rather than crowded, and welcoming rather than commercial.
